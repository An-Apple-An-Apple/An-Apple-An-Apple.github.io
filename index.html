Computer graphics have been in use since the inception of modern computers, both software and hardware.

Computer graphics is a vast field that governs the presentation and communication of information from the computer to humans such that they return displayable results.

With the advancement of algorithms associated with both two-dimensional and three-dimensional graphical presentations, developers can deploy more powerful usages of computer graphics.

Computer graphics algorithms accompanied by the acceleration of dedicated hardware allows computers to simulate and calculate seemingly impossible inquiries due to the complexity and quantity of calculation.

The calculations and simulation performed in fields of computer graphics can prevent disastrous events by predicting the future movement of natural or artificial disasters by simulation and aid the development of artificial intelligence and other fields with virtual design tools.
